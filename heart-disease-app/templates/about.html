Model & Method

This page documents exactly what is running behind the screening tool, reproduced from the original dissertation notebook.

Dataset

1,319 patient records with 8 features: age, gender, heart rate (impulse), systolic/diastolic blood pressure, blood glucose, CK⁠-⁠MB, and troponin. Target label: negative / positive for heart disease indication.

Pipeline

  1. 80/20 train-test split, random_state=42
  2. Three base classifiers trained independently:
    • Random Forest (100 trees)
    • Logistic Regression
    • SVM (linear kernel, probability estimates enabled)
  3. Combined via a soft-voting VotingClassifier — predictions are the class-probability average of all three models.
